What We'd Love to Hear About

We're a small operation with a big love for college basketball, and community feedback helps us prioritize what to build next. Here are some things we'd especially love to hear about:

  • Data corrections: If you find a stat, record, or piece of information that's wrong, please let us know with a source so we can fix it
  • Feature requests: What would make Hoopsipedia more useful for you? New pages, tools, or data you'd like to see?
  • Missing content: Is there a team, player, season, or topic we should cover that we haven't gotten to yet?
  • General feedback: How's the site working for you? What do you like? What could be better?

Response time: We read every email and do our best to respond within 48 hours. During March Madness, it might take a little longer — you know how it is.
