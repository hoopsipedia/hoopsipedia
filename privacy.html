Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Quick Summary

We use Google Analytics to understand how people use the site. We don't sell your data. We don't collect personal info unless you email us. Cookies are for analytics only.

Analytics

Hoopsipedia uses Google Analytics to see how many people visit, which pages they look at, how long they stay, that kind of thing. This helps us understand what's working and what needs improvement. Google Analytics doesn't collect names or personal details, just anonymized usage data.

Data We Don't Collect

We don't ask you to create accounts. We don't collect personal information just for browsing the site. If you email us using the contact page, we'll have your email address, but we're not selling it or sharing it with anyone.

Cookies

We use cookies for analytics purposes only. These help Google Analytics track site usage. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you want, though some site features might not work as well.

Third Party Services

We use these services that collect data:

  • Google Analytics - Tracks site traffic and usage patterns
  • ESPN API - Pulls live score data for college basketball
  • SeatGeek - Links to ticket information for games

Our Data Sources

The college basketball data on Hoopsipedia comes from public sources like NCAA recordbooks, Sports Reference, and ESPN. This is all public information that we're organizing in one place.

Not Affiliated

Hoopsipedia is an independent project and is not affiliated with the NCAA, ESPN, any conference, or any university. We're just a basketball fan building a resource.
