About Hoopsipedia

What is Hoopsipedia?

Hoopsipedia is a passion project built by a college basketball fan who got tired of hunting through the internet for solid program history and season-by-season data. This site covers 360+ Division I programs with everything from historical records and tournament appearances to coaching tenures and head-to-head comparisons.

What You'll Find Here

We track NCAA Tournament history, season records, tournament results, coaching records, program comparisons, and historical standings. Whether you want to see how a team's performed over the last 50 years or check out a coach's record at multiple schools, we've got you covered.

Our data is sourced from Sports Reference for historical season data, ESPN for live scores and current standings, and SeatGeek for ticket availability. Our ultimate source of truth for all-time records, wins, and program achievements is the NCAA official record book. This means vacated wins are not counted in our all-time totals, ensuring our data reflects each program's official standing.

Our Goal

Make Hoopsipedia the go-to resource for anyone who wants to understand college basketball history. No clickbait, no fluff. Just solid data about the teams and players who've shaped the game over the decades.
