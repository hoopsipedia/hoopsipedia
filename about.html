About Hoopsipedia

The free college basketball encyclopedia

What Is Hoopsipedia?

Hoopsipedia is a free, comprehensive college basketball reference and encyclopedia. Our mission is to make college basketball history, statistics, records, and information accessible to every fan — from the casual viewer tuning in for March Madness to the die-hard follower who lives and breathes the sport year-round.

We cover NCAA Division I men's and women's college basketball, including team histories, conference records, tournament results, player profiles, coaching legacies, rivalry breakdowns, and much more.

Our goal is simple: be the most useful, most comprehensive, and most fan-friendly college basketball resource on the internet — and keep it completely free for everyone.

Why We Built This

College basketball has one of the richest histories in American sports, but finding detailed, well-organized information about it shouldn't require digging through dozens of different websites. Hoopsipedia was created to bring it all together in one place — the teams, the players, the moments, and the numbers that define the game.

Whether you're settling a debate with friends, researching your team's all-time record, or just exploring the history of the sport, Hoopsipedia is built to help you find what you're looking for quickly and easily.

What You'll Find Here

  • Team Profiles: Detailed pages for NCAA Division I programs covering all-time records, conference history, notable seasons, and key achievements
  • Historical Data: Tournament results, season recaps, coaching records, and conference standings spanning decades of college basketball
  • Head-to-Head Matchups: Rivalry data and historical series records between programs
  • Player & Coach Information: Profiles of the players and coaches who shaped the game
  • Stats & Records: Comprehensive statistical breakdowns and record books

Who's Behind Hoopsipedia?

Hoopsipedia was created by a lifelong college basketball fan who wanted to build the resource he wished already existed. The site is independently operated and is not affiliated with the NCAA, any conference, or any university.

We are committed to accuracy and are always working to expand and improve our content. If you spot an error or have a suggestion, we'd love to hear from you on our Contact page.

Always Free

Hoopsipedia is and will always be free to use. The site is supported by non-intrusive display advertising, which helps cover the costs of running and maintaining the site. We will never use pop-up ads, full-screen takeovers, or anything that gets in the way of your experience.
